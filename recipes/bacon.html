Crispy Bacon

Description

Crispy bacon is a savory, crispy treat that can be served as a side dish or incorporated into a variety of meals. It adds a delicious, smoky flavor and satisfying crunch to everything from salads to burgers.

Ingredients

  • Bacon strips: Use thick-cut bacon for the best texture and flavor.
  • Optional seasonings: Black pepper, brown sugar, or chili powder for extra flavor.

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C) for even cooking.
  2. Place the bacon strips on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Optionally, sprinkle with black pepper or other seasonings.
  4. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the bacon reaches your desired level of crispness.
  5. Carefully remove the bacon and drain on paper towels.
  6. Serve immediately or store in an airtight container for later use.
