Fresh Garden Salad

Description

Fresh Garden Salad is a colorful, healthy dish made from a variety of crisp, fresh vegetables and topped with a light vinaigrette. It's the perfect addition to any meal or can stand alone as a light, refreshing lunch.

Ingredients

  • Mixed greens: Romaine lettuce, arugula, spinach, and kale.
  • Vegetables: Cucumbers, tomatoes, carrots, and bell peppers.
  • Olives: A mix of black and green olives adds a briny flavor.
  • Cheese: Crumbled feta or goat cheese adds a tangy richness.
  • Croutons: A crunchy element to balance the salad.
  • Vinaigrette: Olive oil, balsamic vinegar, mustard, salt, and pepper.

Steps

  1. Wash and chop all the vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the mixed greens, vegetables, and olives.
  3. Add the crumbled cheese and croutons on top.
  4. Drizzle with vinaigrette and toss to combine.
  5. Serve immediately, or chill in the fridge for 15 minutes before serving.
